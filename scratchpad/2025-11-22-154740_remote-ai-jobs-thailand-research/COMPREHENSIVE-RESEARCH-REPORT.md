### 2. LEGAL & TAX FRAMEWORK

**Visa Strategy:**
- **DTV (Destination Thailand Visa):** 5-year validity, $13,650 savings requirement, NO work permit needed for foreign employers
- **LTR Visa:** 10-year option if earning $80,000+/year
- **Work Permit:** NOT required for remote work with overseas employers

**Tax Optimization:**
- **<180 days/year in Thailand** = Avoid Thai tax residency entirely
- **<16 days/year in UK** = Avoid UK tax residency
- **UK Limited Company structure:** 19-25% corp tax + 8.75% dividend tax (vs 45% sole trader)
- **Use EOR services** ($200-300/month) for complex compliance if needed

**DTA Protection:**
- UK-Thailand Double Taxation Agreement protects you
- If employer has NO permanent establishment in Thailand AND you spend <183 days = exempt from Thai tax
- Foreign tax credits prevent double taxation
