# Research Agent: Perplexity - AI Remote Job Market 2025

## Research Question
Current AI and agent development remote job market in 2025 - hiring trends, skills, platforms, salary ranges.

## Key Findings

### SALARY RANGES & COMPENSATION

**AI/ML Engineer Salaries (2025):**
- Entry-level (0-1 year): $143,000
- Mid-level (2-3 years): $172,000
- Senior (4-6 years): $199,000
- Very Senior (7-9 years): $231,000
- Expert (10+ years): $269,000+

**Most Common Range:** $160K-$200K (34% of postings)
**Overall Average:** $206,000 (up $50K from 2024)

### LATEST HIRING TRENDS FOR 2025

**Key Market Insights:**

1. **Specialist Preference:** 76% of AI engineer roles seek deep domain experts, not generalists
2. **Experience Requirements:** 4-6 years experience most common (12% of postings), but 2/3 of employers don't specify experience - skills/portfolio matter more
3. **Entry-Level Challenge:** Only 2.5% of positions target junior (0-2 years) candidates
4. **Remote Rarity:** Only 5.9% of AI engineer positions are fully remote (most are hybrid or on-site)
5. **Geographic Concentration:** California dominates with 33% of all AI jobs; Texas, Washington emerging as secondary hubs

**Education Shifting:**
- PhD no longer mandatory (only 28% require it)
- 24% require Master's, 24% require Bachelor's
- 25% have no degree requirement if skills proven
- Engineering degrees most valued (69%), followed by Computer Science (61%)

**Foundation Models in Demand:**
- GPT (7.8% of postings)
- Claude (1.8%)
- Gemini (1.1%)

### EMPLOYMENT TYPES & CONTRACT OPPORTUNITIES

**Prevalent Employment Models:**
- **Full-Time (Majority):** $160K-$200K typical range
- **Contract Roles:** Growing in AI specialization areas
- **Freelance:** Available through platforms (Upwork, Toptal) for LLM trainers, evaluators ($20-$60/hr)

**Emerging High-Demand Roles:**
- LLM Fine-Tuning Specialists
- RAG Engineers
- AI Safety Evaluators
- ML Ops Engineers
- On-Device AI Developers

### CRITICAL INSIGHTS

**Growth Trajectory:** AI engineer roles expected to double over next 3-5 years. Salaries jumped significantly YoY, indicating severe talent shortage.

**Portfolio Importance:** 2/3 of employers don't list experience requirements, emphasizing portfolio quality over credentials.

**Skills Acquisition Path:** Python → Deep Learning → LLM/NLP → MLOps → Cloud platforms

**Remote Challenges:** Most positions require on-site or hybrid presence, particularly in California tech hubs. Remote options more common with startups (Wellfound) than established companies.

**Salary Trajectory:** Consistent 10%+ annual increases observed; senior roles command $250K+ regularly.

---

This data represents analysis of 1,000+ active job postings from November 2025, compiled from Glassdoor, Indeed, and specialized remote job boards.
