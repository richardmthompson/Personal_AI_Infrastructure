# Research Agent: Perplexity - Companies Hiring Remote Thailand

## Research Question

**Top Job Platforms Specializing in Thailand Remote Work:**
- Arc.dev - Global tech companies hiring remote engineers
- Crossover.com - US software companies with remote Bangkok positions
- Remote Rocketship - 234+ software engineer remote jobs in Thailand
- JobsDB Thailand - 131 remote developer jobs listed
- Indeed - 25+ flexible remote software positions
- Wellfound - Startup and tech jobs with remote options
- MeetFrank - 64 fintech remote jobs in Thailand
- Himalayas.app - Remote company profiles in Thailand

4. **Full Stack Development** - Frontend, Backend, DevOps, QA roles
