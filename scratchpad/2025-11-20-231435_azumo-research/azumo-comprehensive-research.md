## 6. HIRING PROCESS & TIMELINE

### Overview
**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks (fast and transparent process)
**Communication:** All via email (@azumo.co or @azumo.com domains)

### Detailed Interview Stages

**Stage 1: Initial HR Interview (45 minutes)**
- Platform: Google Meet
- Topics covered:
  - Company information and culture
  - Your interests and career goals
  - Your experience and background
  - Salary expectations
  - English language validation (for non-native speakers)
- Interviewer: HR team member

**Stage 2: Technical Assessment**
- Delivered through Test Gorilla platform
- Via same email thread from initial contact
- **Format:** 3 coding exercises in 1.5 hours
- **Topics:** Backend, frontend, language-specific questions
- **Tip:** Practice coding challenges on LeetCode, HackerRank beforehand

**Stage 3: Technical Interview**
- Only after passing technical assessment
- Meet with:
  - Technical Leader
  - Product Owner (sometimes)
- Deep dive into your technical experience
- Potentially live coding or architecture discussions
- Questions about AI/ML projects, deployment experience

**Stage 4: Offer**
- If you pass technical interview, formal offer is extended
- Typically within same week of final interview

### Interview Difficulty
- **Glassdoor Rating:** 2.7/5 (moderate difficulty)
- **Candidate Experience:** 50% rate as positive
- **Focus Areas:** Technical competency, cultural fit, communication skills
