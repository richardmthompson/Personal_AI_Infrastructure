## Content Stream Strategy

**Core principle**: You're not "creating content"‚Äîyou're **documenting work you're already doing**.

Each pillar = ongoing narrative stream:

- **Vox Manifestor Diaries** (weekly/bi-weekly progress)
- **Cognitive Ecology Video Series** (monthly deep-dives)
- **ResearchBot Chronicles** (as you build/improve it)
- **VoxPlan Evolution** (meta-analysis of tool iteration)
- **2 Years Retrospective** (structured mini-series)

### Why These Pillars Work

1. **Project-based** ‚Üí never run out of content (document actual work)
2. **Interconnected** ‚Üí voice + cognition + agents + goals = Vox Systems vision
3. **Narrative arcs** ‚Üí incomplete projects becoming complete = compelling story
4. **Differentiation** ‚Üí nobody else has this exact intersection of expertise
5. **Aligned with business** ‚Üí every post builds toward Vox Systems Inc.
6. **Radically honest** ‚Üí messy truth, not highlight reel (old school hacker ethos)
